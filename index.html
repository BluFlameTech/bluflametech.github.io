DEVELOPER-ORIENTED

No YAP (Yet Another Portal) app here! Everything (and we mean EVERYTHING) is available in the developer's workspace. From configuration to logs to build triggers, build results and more. We believe that an essential part of DevOps is enabling developers, which is why that guides everything we do.

SIMPLE

Some CI/CD automation tools claim to be simple. Our CodeZero CI/CD automation actually is. With just a Git repository, many projects can be setup using a single operation in minutes. Even if you don't know how to get started, we've got you covered with examples and proven workflows to get your project up and running today.

GOT TOOLS?

If you already have tools that you know and love, no problem! You can use them with our CodeZero CI/CD automation product. We may even have built-in integrations for them. If not, you can easily add your own integrations. If you don't have your own tools or if you're not sure which tools you should use, we've got you covered there, too.

EXTENDABLE

We can get your project bootstrapped quickly using intelligent defaults and our battle tested workflows. But there may come a time when you need something a little extra. That's OK, we get it. That's why nearly everything is extendable. From workflows to tool integrations to project archetypes and more, our CodeZero CI/CD automation product grows with you.

FREE FOR OPEN SOURCE

We believe in open source. That's why CodeZero by Blu Flame Technologies is 100% free for open source projects and it always will be. We want you to build high quality open source software and we think our CodeZero CI/CD automation product is the best product to help you do that.

END-TO-END AUTOMATION

Our CodeZero CI/CD automation product is everything you need for end-to-end automation and nothing you don't. Create new [working] projects, integrate with confidence, build, test, scan, deploy... Get up and running in minutes, use the tools you love and adjust as you grow.
